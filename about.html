About Us

Empowering manga readers worldwide with innovative technology

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of developers, designers, and manga enthusiasts dedicated to creating the ultimate manga reading experience. Our platform was born from a simple idea: manga reading should be accessible, customizable, and enjoyable for everyone.

Since our inception, we've grown into a thriving community of millions of users worldwide, all united by their love for manga and anime culture. We believe that great stories deserve great reading experiences, and that's exactly what we deliver.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize manga reading by providing a free, open-source platform that puts users first. We're committed to:

  • User Privacy: We never track your reading habits or sell your data. Your privacy is non-negotiable.
  • Open Source Development: Our code is transparent and community-driven, ensuring quality and trust.
  • Continuous Innovation: We constantly improve and add features based on user feedback and emerging technologies.
  • Accessibility: Everyone should have access to manga, regardless of their location or financial situation.
  • Community First: Our users are at the heart of everything we do, from feature development to support.
